## Starter Quests — "First Week in ProsperitySMP"

Every new player has access to a six-quest onboarding chain. Quests are posted on the bulletin board at Town Hall. Complete them in order, at your own pace.

### Quest 1 — Welcome Aboard

**Objective:** Visit Town Hall and pick up your Welcome Packet from the chest near the entrance.

**Reward:** Iron pickaxe, iron axe, 8× bread, $10 city stipend

The iron tools get you past wooden gear immediately. The Welcome Packet chest is restocked by staff — if it's empty, message an admin.

---

### Quest 2 — Pick a Trade

**Objective:** Join a job using `/jobs join <jobname>`.

Use `/jobs browse` to see all six options: Miner, Farmer, Builder, Woodcutter, Hunter, Fisher.

**Reward:** $25 city stipend + a job starter kit

| Job | Starter Kit |
|---|---|
| Miner | 64× torches, 1× bucket |
| Farmer | 1× hoe, 64× wheat seeds, 16× bone meal |
| Builder | 32× stone, 32× oak planks, 1× shovel |
| Woodcutter | 1× extra iron axe, 4× oak saplings |
| Hunter | 1× iron sword, 16× arrows, 1× bow |
| Fisher | 1× fishing rod |

---

### Quest 3 — First Shift

**Objective:** Earn $50 in job wages — the direct cash from your job, not item sales.

**Reward:** $50 city stipend, 1× City Work Permit (collectible)

This takes 15–30 minutes depending on your job. The Work Permit is a cosmetic collectible — it has no mechanical function, but it's yours.

---

### Quest 4 — Sell Something

**Objective:** Make any sale — to the admin shop or to another player.

**Reward:** $35 city stipend

The admin shop is the **price floor**, not the target. Selling to other players earns more. This quest introduces both options.

---

### Quest 5 — Take a Contract

**Objective:** Complete one public contract using the `/deliver` command (see the next section).

**Reward:** The stated contract payout + $25 city completion bonus + 1× City Commendation

The Commendation is a cosmetic collectible at launch. Future updates may use commendation count as a gate for higher-tier contracts.

---

### Quest 6 — Stake Your Claim

**Objective:** Rent a residential plot or a market stall.

**Reward:** First week's rent waived (city covers it as a settlement bonus) + welcome sign kit

A plot is a reason to log back in.

## Public Contracts — The Contract Board

The Town Hall **Contract Board** has 12 standing contracts you can fulfill any time. Bring the requested items, run `/deliver <id>`, and the city pays you on the spot. No admin verification needed — it's all automated.

<InfoCallout type="info" title="Contracts are bonus income, not your main income">
  Contracts pay above the admin floor — better than dumping to the server — but below player market peaks. They give you steady supplementary income with daily caps to prevent farming. Use them between bigger projects, or chain them while leveling.
</InfoCallout>

### How to Use the Contract Board

1. Visit the **Contract Board** outside Town Hall
2. Read the sign for the contract you want
3. Make sure the items are in your inventory
4. Run `/deliver <id>` — for example, `/deliver coal64`
5. The city takes the items and pays you instantly

Other useful commands:
- `/contracts` — list all contracts available
- `/contractinfo <id>` — show full details for one contract (including your cooldown and daily count)

### Maximum Daily Income from the Board

If you fully clear every contract every day, the daily cap totals:

| Tier | Total contracts available | Theoretical max payout |
|---|---|---|
| Cobble/Stone (high cap) | 20 fills | $1,300 |
| Wood/Coal/Wool/Torch | 30 fills | $2,800 |
| Food | 15 fills | $1,400 |
| Iron/Diamond (high value) | 7 fills | $1,400 |
| **Total daily cap** | **~72 fills** | **~$6,900/day** |

This is a hard ceiling. The board is for steady income, not unlimited grinding.

## Construction Contracts — Staff-Verified

Bigger physical builds — paths, fences, signage — are posted separately and verified by staff after completion.

| ID | Contract | Required | Payout |
|---|---|---|---|
| C-08 | Path Paving: Market District | ~200 blocks, staff-directed | $200 |
| C-09 | Agricultural District Fencing | ~300 fence blocks, materials provided | $175 |
| C-10 | Town Signage | 8 signs at staff-marked posts, signs provided | $80 |

<InfoCallout type="info" title="For Skilled Builders: Government Commissions">
  The contracts above are the **entry tier** for the Builder career. Players who consistently deliver quality work on these smaller commissions are invited to bid on **government commissions** — major civic landmarks, expansion districts, and the planned **dungeon zone on the mountain**. These are negotiated directly with the server owner. Pay is per-project and scales with scope — $10,000+ is realistic for high-skill builds.

  **City contracts include materials** — you're paid for labor and design. Private builds (for other players, on their plots) are a separate market and materials are between you and the client.
</InfoCallout>

## Contract Tiers

| Tier | Time Investment | Payout Range |
|---|---|---|
| Contract Board (automated) | A few minutes per `/deliver` | $50–$200 per fill |
| Beginner Construction | ~30 min | $80–$200 |
| Standard Construction | 1–2 hours | $200–$600 |
| Large Construction | 2–4 hours | $600–$1,500 |
| Infrastructure | Multiple sessions | $1,500–$5,000 + title/naming rights |
| **Government Commission** *(Builder, invite-only)* | Multi-session, owner-directed | **$10,000+ negotiated** |

Tier 2+ contracts will be added over time as the board matures. Government commissions are a Builder-specific track — the server owner invites high-quality builders directly.

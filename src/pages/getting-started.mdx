## First 30 Minutes

1. **Read the rules.** Visit Town Hall or read the [full rules](/rules) before doing anything else.
2. **Pick a job.** Run `/jobs browse` to see all available jobs, then `/jobs join <jobname>` to join one. You start with 3 free job slots.
3. **Get your starter kit.** You spawn with a wooden pickaxe, wooden axe, and 8 bread. Use them.
4. **Go earn money.** Mine, chop, farm, fish, or hunt in the resource world — whatever matches your job. Your first iron pickaxe costs around $80–$120 and takes 10–20 minutes to earn.
5. **Visit the market.** Get a feel for prices, supply gaps, and what other players are selling.
6. **Check the public contracts board at Town Hall.** Contracts pay well and give you goals beyond raw grinding.
7. **Rent starter housing or save for a plot.** You need a base. Starter housing is available near spawn.

## Good First Goals

- Earn your first **$1,000**
- Buy or rent starter housing
- Complete a basic public contract
- Upgrade from wooden to iron tools
- Decide which job to level to 50

## Jobs — How They Work

Jobs are how you earn wages and build a profession identity. You join them via `/jobs join` and leave via `/jobs leave`.

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| Starting job slots | 3 free |
| 4th slot | $10,000 career expansion permit |
| Max level per job | 50 |
| Time to max one job | ~50 hours |
| Income bonus | +1% per level, max +50% at level 50 |

## The 6 Jobs

| Job | How You Earn | Key Late-Game |
|---|---|---|
| **Miner** | Break ores and stone underground | Efficiency VII pickaxe, Fortune IV/V, mining drills |
| **Farmer** | Harvest mature crops manually | Farming tractors, biofuel, large farm contracts |
| **Builder** | Place building materials and complete build contracts | Government commissions for major civic builds and dungeon zones — negotiated pay for the best builders |
| **Woodcutter** | Fell trees manually | Treated lumber, forestry contracts, bulk supply deals |
| **Hunter** | Kill hostile mobs | Bounty contracts, elite mob hunts, rare drop market |
| **Fisher** | Fish manually with a rod | Rare catch contracts, ocean expedition commissions |

You can hold up to 3 jobs at once. Specializing in one gives you better titles, contracts, and tools for that job. Holding multiple jobs gives you more income sources and business flexibility. Both are valid.

## How Income Works

Your hourly income comes from four places — not just your job wage:

| Source | Description |
|---|---|
| **Job wage** | Direct cash paid by Jobs Reborn when you take an action |
| **Item sales to admin shop** | Sell your drops at the admin shop price floor |
| **Player market** | Sell to other players for more than the admin floor |
| **Contracts** | Bonus payouts from completing public or private contracts |

At level 1, expect **$125–$175/hr** total across all four sources. At level 50, expect **$225–$275/hr** from the same job — plus significantly more if you unlock drills, Efficiency VII tools, or contract access.

## Important Rules to Know

- **No AFK farms.** Fully automatic farms are banned. You must be present and interacting.
- **No griefing.** Other players' land and builds are protected by WorldGuard.
- **No exploits or duplication.** Report them. Using them results in a ban.
- **In-game crimes are handled by the law system**, not staff. Theft, assault, and fraud go through courts and fines, not bans.

Read the [full rules](/rules) and [laws](/laws) before your first session.

## What This Server Uses

No mods or client-side installs required. Everything runs server-side. Here's what's powering the gameplay:

| Feature | What It Does |
|---|---|
| **Job System** (Jobs Reborn) | Earn wages for mining, farming, building, and more. Level up to 50 per job. |
| **Skill Tree** (custom) | Earn passive perks as your job levels up — extra drops, better tools, expanded reach, durability protection. Open with `/skills`. |
| **Player Shops** (ChestShop) | Any player can set up a chest shop with a sign. The player market is the real economy. |
| **Land Protection** (WorldGuard + AreaShop) | Your plot is fully protected. Buy or rent land through signs at each plot. |
| **Drills & Tractors** (Vehicles) | Buy mining drills and farming tractors with in-game money. Use them in the resource world only. |
| **Custom Fishing** (EvenMoreFish) | Catch rare fish with sell prices in the resource world. Skill tree upgrades boost rarity rolls. |
| **Multi-World Travel** (Multiverse + BetterRTP) | Separate main world, resource world, nether, and end. `/rtp` drops you safely in the resource world. |
| **Job Titles in Chat & Tab** (LuckPerms + TAB) | Your highest-tier job title shows in chat, tab list, and nametag. Use `/title` to pin a specific job's title. |
| **In-World Holograms** (DecentHolograms) | Floating text labels for shops, plots, contract boards, and points of interest around the city. |
| **Smart Item Stacking** (RoseStacker) | Dropped items merge on the ground to reduce lag. Mob and block stacking are intentionally disabled — only item drops stack. |
| **Live Web Map** (BlueMap) | Browse the world online at the map URL — find your way around without launching the game. |
| **No Mending** | The Mending enchantment is intentionally not available on this server. Tool care is a real cost — see Axe Care, Rod Care, and Block Recovery skills. |
| **Grief Logging & Rollback** | Every block placed, broken, and chest accessed is logged. Staff can roll back grief to any point. Reporting an incident usually takes a few minutes to resolve. |

## Refer a Friend — Both of You Get Paid

If you invite a friend who sticks around, the city pays you both. It's a thank-you for growing the community.

**How it works:**
1. Your friend joins the server (whitelist them via Discord first)
2. When they log in for the first time, they DM staff: *"\<your name\> invited me"*
3. Once your friend hits **2 hours of playtime** and has **joined at least one job**, both of you get the reward

**The rewards:**

| You receive | They receive |
|---|---|
| **$300** + the `[Recruiter]` title | **$150** bonus on top of the $50 starting balance |

**Milestone bonuses** (your invites add up over time):

| Successful invites | Title | Bonus payout |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | `[Recruiter]` | — |
| 3 | `[Veteran]` | +$500 |
| 5 | `[Patron]` (max) | +$1,000 + naming rights on a community billboard |

**Fine print:**
- Cap at **5 successful invites per player** — keeps the system fair
- Invites from alt accounts (same IP) don't count
- The 2-hour playtime + job requirement means we only pay for invites who actually engage with the server
- Rewards are processed manually at launch — there may be a short delay (usually under a day) while staff verifies. We're building an automated version for post-launch.

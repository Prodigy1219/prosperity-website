Welcome to New Prosperity. Your first goal is simple: learn the town, earn your first money, and choose a direction.

## First 30 Minutes

1. **Read the rules at Town Hall.** Rules exist in-game as books. Read them before doing anything else.
2. **Choose a starter job.** Talk to the job board or NPC at Town Hall. Pick something that matches how you want to play.
3. **Visit the market.** Get a feel for prices, supply gaps, and what other players are selling.
4. **Check the public contracts board.** Public contracts are the fastest way to earn your first money.
5. **Gather resources in the resource world.** The resource world resets regularly. Mine and gather here, not the main world.
6. **Earn your first money.** Complete a contract, sell resources, or provide a service.
7. **Rent starter housing or save for a starter plot.** You need a base. Housing is available near spawn.
8. **Pick your first profession path.** Once you have starter funds, commit to a direction.

## Good First Goals

- Earn your first **$1,000**
- Buy or rent starter housing
- Complete a basic public contract
- Visit every major Town Hall office
- Decide whether you want to become a **Miner, Farmer, Builder, Merchant, Industrialist**, or **Civil Service** worker

## Profession Paths

Once you have a starter job and some money, commit to a skill tree. Each path unlocks unique abilities and licenses.

| Profession | Focus |
|---|---|
| **Miner** | Resource extraction, underground exploration, fuel systems |
| **Farmer** | Food production, biofuel, crop optimization |
| **Builder** | Construction, roads, architectural licenses |
| **Merchant** | Shops, trading, company formation |
| **Industrialist** | Semi-automatic production, machinery, factories |
| **Civil Service** | Government, law enforcement, public works |

Read the full [Skill Trees guide](/skill-trees) for details on each path.

## Important Rules to Know

- **No AFK farms.** Fully automatic farms are banned. Meaningful player interaction is required.
- **No griefing.** Other players' builds and land are protected.
- **No exploits or duplication.** Report them. Using them results in a ban.
- **In-game crimes are handled by the law system**, not staff. Staff handles real rule violations.

Read the [full rules](/rules) before your first session.

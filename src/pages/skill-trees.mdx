## Overview

New Prosperity has two connected progression systems: **Jobs** and **Skill Trees**.

- **Jobs** earn wages, have levels (1–50), and grant titles and profession unlocks as you level up. You join them via `/jobs join`. They consume a job slot.
- **Skill Trees** are planned buff and unlock paths — additional layers on top of your job progression. Not yet implemented at launch.

<InfoCallout type="warning" title="Under Construction">
  Skill tree buff values and specific unlock costs are subject to change before launch. The job progression system is active — the skill tree buff layer is planned for after the economy stabilizes.
</InfoCallout>

## Job Slots

| Slot | Cost |
|---|---|
| Slot 1 | Free |
| Slot 2 | Free |
| Slot 3 | ~$500 early unlock |
| Slot 4 | $10,000 career expansion permit |
| Slot 5 | Prestige / late-season unlock |

## Specialization vs Diversification

Both paths are valid — the system is designed so neither feels wrong.

| Path | What You Get |
|---|---|
| **Specialize** (go deep in one job) | Stronger wage scaling, better titles, better contracts, better tool access, late-game unlocks |
| **Diversify** (level multiple jobs) | More business models, more income sources, more flexibility |

A level 50 Miner is the best miner on the server. A player with Miner + Builder + Woodcutter has more options. Pick based on how you want to play.

## The 6 Jobs

Each job has 50 levels, takes ~50 hours to max, and grants a +1% income bonus per level (max +50% at level 50 applied to job wage). Every 10 levels unlocks a new chat title. Every 5 levels gives a real perk or access unlock.

## Prestige

When you reach level 50 in a job:

- You earn **Prestige** status in that profession
- You receive a unique permanent chat title
- You gain access to late-game systems exclusive to prestige players
- You get a named contribution plaque in your profession's district
- You become eligible for the highest-tier contracts on the server

Reaching Prestige takes real in-game time and investment. It is the late-game.

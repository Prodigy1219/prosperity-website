## Overview

Skill trees give players a **profession identity**. Each path unlocks unique abilities, licenses, tools, and vehicles that define your role in the economy.

Skill trees combine **major unlocks** with **small utility buffs**:

- **Major unlocks**: Licenses, vehicles, land access, custom enchants, company rights, machinery
- **Small buffs**: Convenience perks that improve your identity without replacing the core economy

<InfoCallout type="warning" title="Season 0 Note">
  Skill tree values, unlock costs, and specific buffs are subject to change during beta. These descriptions reflect the intended design direction.
</InfoCallout>

## Citizen Core

Every player starts at **Citizen** level. It unlocks:

- Basic job access
- Ability to rent housing
- Access to the public market
- The right to complete basic public contracts

---

## Miner

**Focus**: Resource extraction, underground operations, fuel systems

### Major Unlocks
- Mining vehicle license (ore cart / drill)
- Access to deep mining districts
- Ore processing equipment
- Industrial fuel contracts

### Skill Buffs
- **Headlamp I** — Permanent light source when underground
- **Ore Sorter** — Automatic sorting of ore drops into inventory categories
- **Portable Smelting I** — Smelt a limited number of ores per session in the field
- **Drill Maintenance** — Reduced vehicle wear rate
- **Fuel Discipline** — Reduced fuel consumption on mining vehicles

---

## Farmer

**Focus**: Food production, crop optimization, biofuel supply

### Major Unlocks
- Agricultural vehicle license (tractor)
- Biofuel production rights
- Large agricultural plot access
- Food supply contracts

### Skill Buffs
- **Green Thumb** — Increased crop yield rate
- **Compost Sense** — Composting produces more bonemeal per cycle
- **Seed Saver** — Chance to retain seeds after harvest
- **Tractor Handling** — Improved tractor speed and fuel efficiency
- **Biofuel Basics** — Unlock basic biofuel crafting recipes

---

## Builder

**Focus**: Construction, infrastructure, architectural licensing

### Major Unlocks
- Architect license (required for public construction contracts)
- Road crew rights
- Scaffold system access
- Large commercial construction contracts

### Skill Buffs
- **Steady Hand** — Reduced item consumption on decorative block placement
- **Scaffold Expert** — Extended scaffold reach
- **Material Planner** — See estimated resource cost of a planned structure
- **Road Crew** — Increased speed on public road contracts
- **Architect License** — Required to bid on major public buildings

---

## Industrialist

**Focus**: Semi-automatic production, machinery, factory operations

### Major Unlocks
- Industrial machinery license
- Factory plot access
- Semi-automatic production rights
- Heavy equipment operator license

### Skill Buffs
- **Process Efficiency** — Increased output per material cycle
- **Machine Calibration** — Reduced machine maintenance costs
- **Circuit Understanding** — Unlock basic redstone automation schematics
- **Shift Planner** — Track production rates per session
- **Fuel Routing** — Optimized fuel routing between machines

---

## Merchant

**Focus**: Trade, shops, companies, and the market

### Major Unlocks
- Merchant license (required to open a shop)
- Company formation rights
- Auction house access (seller)
- Trade route contracts

### Skill Buffs
- **Market Read** — See basic price trend indicators
- **Bulk Discount** — Reduced cost on large admin shop purchases
- **Display Case** — Enhanced shop display for featured items
- **Trade Network** — Access to inter-player trade board
- **Company Registry** — Ability to register a company and hire staff

---

## Civil Service

**Focus**: Government, law enforcement, public works, city administration

### Major Unlocks
- Law enforcement badge (with election/appointment)
- City council candidacy rights
- Public contract administration
- Court system participation rights

### Skill Buffs
- **Due Process** — Extended time window on legal procedures
- **Record Access** — View city records and property ownership logs
- **Budget Planner** — Access to city treasury reports
- **Public Speaker** — Reduced cooldown on council motions
- **Civic Trust** — Reputation bonus with city NPCs

---

## Prestige and Development Influence

When you max out your primary skill tree:

- You earn **Prestige** status in your profession
- You gain access to **development influence** — the ability to propose and vote on city expansion projects
- Prestige players drive world border expansions and major construction

Reaching Prestige takes real in-game time and investment. It is the late-game.
